Cowspiracy

This was one of the first documentaries i watch about veganism, it talks about so many things we might not even think about. Here are some statements from the documentary:

  • Animal agriculture is responsible for 18 percent of greenhouse gas emissions, more than the combined exhaust from all transportation.
  • 90-100 million tons of fish are pulled from our oceans each year.
  • The leading causes of rainforest destruction are livestock and feedcrops.
